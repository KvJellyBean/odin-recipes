Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish known for its layers of rich, savory flavors. It typically consists of wide flat pasta sheets layered with a delicious mixture of cheese, sauce, and often includes ground meat or vegetables. The entire dish is then baked until it's bubbling and golden, creating a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 450g Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion Finely Chopped
  • 2 Clover Garlic
  • 1 Can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 Tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 Teaspoon Dried Basil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 425g Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Cups Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add the chopped onion and garlic and sauté until they become translucent.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 15 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 1 cup of shredded mozzarella, 1/4 cup grated Parmesan, one egg, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, then drain.
  6. To assemble the lasagna, spread a small amount of the meat sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Place a layer of noodles on top.
  7. Spread a portion of the cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of meat sauce. Repeat the layers, finishing with a top layer of meat sauce.
  8. Sprinkle the remaining 1 cup of shredded mozzarella and 1/4 cup grated Parmesan on top.
  9. Cover with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the top is bubbly and golden.
  11. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your simple homemade lasagna!
