Rendang

Description

Rendang is a highly flavorful and aromatic Indonesian dish, specifically hailing from the Minangkabau ethnic group of West Sumatra. It's often regarded as one of the most delicious and iconic dishes in Indonesian cuisine. Rendang is a type of dry curry characterized by its rich, tender, and intensely spiced meat, typically made with beef, although variations with chicken or other proteins can be found.

Ingredients

  • 700g Beef Stew Meat
  • 2 Cans Coconut Milk
  • 2 Kaffir Lime Leaves
  • Salt
  • 1 Onion
  • 3 Clover Garlic
  • 1-inch Piece of Ginger
  • 1-inch Piece of Galangal
  • 2 Lemongrass Stalks
  • 4-6 Dried Red Chilies
  • 1 Teaspoon Ground Turmeric
  • 1-2 Tablespoons Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. In a food processor, blend all the spice paste ingredients (onion, garlic, ginger, galangal, lemongrass, dried chilies, and turmeric) into a smooth paste. You can add a little water if needed to facilitate blending.
  2. In a large, heavy-bottomed pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the spice paste and saut√© it for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes fragrant and the oil starts to separate.
  3. Add the beef chunks to the pot and cook, stirring, until they are lightly browned on all sides.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and add the kaffir lime leaves (if using). Stir well.
  5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer for at least 2-3 hours, or until the meat becomes very tender and the sauce thickens. Be sure to stir occasionally to prevent sticking and check for seasoning, adding salt to taste.
  6. As the rendang simmers, the sauce will gradually reduce, and the meat will absorb the rich flavors of the spices and coconut milk. Continue to cook until the sauce becomes thick and the meat is tender and fully flavored.
  7. Once the rendang reaches your desired consistency and the meat is tender, remove it from heat.
  8. Serve your homemade rendang with steamed rice. Enjoy the rich and aromatic flavors of this classic Indonesian dish!
